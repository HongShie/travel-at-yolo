WHY FRANCE?

The French Alps are home to many superb ski resorts and chalets, the first of which was established in the early 20th century. Today it remains one of the premier winter sports regions, popular with ski enthusiasts from all over the world.

Our Take

France is a culturally rich country with a profound impact on art, cuisine, fashion, and history. It has produced influential artists, writers, and thinkers throughout its history. The country's cuisine is celebrated globally, and it's known for its sophistication and elegance.

France's historical legacy includes periods of monarchy, revolution, and republicanism, notably the French Revolution of 1789. Iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral adorn its landscape. French is the official language, and the country's economy is diverse, encompassing industries like aerospace, automotive, and luxury goods. France has a semi-presidential system of government and is a member of the European Union, playing a significant role in international affairs. Its emphasis on social welfare is evident through its healthcare system and social safety net.
