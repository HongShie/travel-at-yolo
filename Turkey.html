WHY TURKEY?

Turquoise Coast is a Mediterranean paradise where you can trace mythological legends, dive the sunken remnants of ancient civilizations, snorkel in crystal clear waters and moor up in a romantic bays or sandy beaches - all awaiting your discovery.

Our Take

Experience the epitome of a Mediterranean paradise with sailing vacations along Turkey's captivating 'Turquoise Coast.' Embark on a voyage of discovery as hidden coves, majestic cliffs, age-old tombs, and inviting sandy beaches unveil themselves to you.

Snorkel in crystal clear waters, dive the sunken remnants of ancient civilisations and moor up in a romantic bay - it's time for you to embark on an unforgettable sail holiday in Turkey, where every moment promises a new adventure and countless memories.

What to look out for

WE LOVE

The 600 miles of spectacular shoreline, turquoise hues, secluded coves, ancient ruins and iconic beaches.

KEY LOCATION

Alanya, Antalya, Bodrum, Fethiye, Kalkan, Kas, Kemer and Marmaris. Ölüdeniz, Patara, İztuzu beaches.

KEY EXPERIENCES

Paragliding at Oludeniz, visit the historical Lycian archeological sites, explore Butterfly Valley, dive amongst sunken ruins of Kekova.
