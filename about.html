We care passionately about what we do, and we count both our customers and our colleagues as friends and family.

Travel has always been an integral part of my life, encompassing cherished moments spent with family, exhilarating adventures with friends, and captivating journeys throughout Africa. It is a true passion of mine. As a teenager, I had the opportunity to win a travel scholarship that allowed me to engage in community work in Africa. This eye-opening experience left a profound impact on me and solidified my desire to pursue a career in travel.
Wong Hong Shie
Founder
I've always had a great connection with travel. My father founded and ran Vacation Work, a travel book publishing company so travel books from ‘Work your Way around the World’ to 'The Kibbutz Volunteer’ and the pioneering Travellers Survival Kit series were always littered around our house. I was also an amateur jockey for some years so I guess the thrill of crossing terrain is instilled in me.
Leong Kang Le
Co-founder

So when you're next juggling family life and work- life and want a helping hand with your holiday, know we're here to help!

Director
